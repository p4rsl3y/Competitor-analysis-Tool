How to use Custom Columns

Define the exact data points you want the AI to extract for each competitor.

  • Name: The column header (e.g., "Pricing").
  • Format (Text): AI writes a short, synthesized summary.
  • Format (Tags): AI classifies the company by strictly selecting from your comma-separated list.
  • Instructions: Direct the AI on what specifically to look for or how to format the output.
